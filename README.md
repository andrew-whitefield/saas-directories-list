## How to use this list

- Pick directories that match your product and audience, not just the biggest names. Twenty relevant listings beat a hundred random ones.
- Submit to a few high-authority review sites for trust and traffic, plus niche directories for links.
- Check whether a listing's outbound link is dofollow or nofollow before you count on it for SEO. Both still help through referral traffic and brand searches.
- Confirm a site is live before investing time. Directories shut down or change, so treat any list as a starting point.

## FAQ

**What is a SaaS directory?**
A website that lists software products so buyers can discover, compare, and review them. Listing your SaaS can earn a backlink, referral traffic, and reviews.

**Are these SaaS directories free?**
Most have a free listing or a free tier. Some add paid plans for featured placement, faster review, or lead generation.

**Do SaaS directories help SEO?**
Yes. They provide early backlinks, referral traffic, branded searches, and presence in the data that AI search engines pull from. They work best alongside editorial links, not instead of them.

**How do I submit my SaaS to these directories?**
Open each link and look for a Submit, Add your product, or Get listed button. Start with the directories most relevant to your category.

**How is this list ordered?**
Alphabetically A to Z, with the recommended first step pinned at the top. Numbers come last.
